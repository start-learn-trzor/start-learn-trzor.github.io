Trezor.io Start® - Starting Up Your Device* | Trezor.io

Trezor.io/start is the official onboarding website for Trezor hardware wallets, guiding users through the process of setting up their devices securely. Whether you're using a Trezor Model T or Trezor One, this guide provides the essential steps to get your hardware wallet ready for use.

 

Step 1: Visit Trezor.io/start

To begin, open your browser and navigate to trezor.io/start. This page provides links to the official Trezor Suite application and detailed instructions for setting up your device. It's important to use this official link to ensure you're downloading authentic software and following secure procedures.

 

Step 2: Download and Install Trezor Suite

Trezor Suite is the desktop application that allows you to manage your cryptocurrencies, update firmware, and access advanced security features. On the Trezor.io/start page, you'll find links to download Trezor Suite for your operating system (Windows, macOS, or Linux). Once downloaded, install the software by following the on-screen instructions.

 

Step 3: Connect Your Trezor Device

After installing Trezor Suite, connect your Trezor device to your computer using the provided USB cable. Trezor Suite will detect the device and prompt you to either set up a new wallet or recover an existing one using your recovery seed.

 

Step 4: Firmware Update

When you connect your Trezor for the first time, it may require a firmware update. This step is crucial for security, as it ensures your device runs the latest software with the most recent security patches. Follow the prompts in Trezor Suite to update your firmware. Ensure your device remains connected throughout the update process.

 

Step 5: Create a New Wallet

If you're setting up a new wallet, Trezor Suite will guide you through the process of creating a recovery seed. This seed is a 12 to 24-word phrase that is crucial for recovering your wallet in case your device is lost or damaged. Write down your recovery seed on the provided card and store it in a safe place. Never share your seed with anyone or store it digitally.

 

Step 6: Secure Your Device

After creating your wallet, you'll be prompted to set a PIN for your Trezor. This PIN adds an extra layer of security, preventing unauthorized access to your device. Choose a strong, memorable PIN and confirm it on your Trezor's screen.

 

Step 7: Start Managing Your Cryptocurrencies

With your Trezor set up, you can now use Trezor Suite to manage your cryptocurrencies. The dashboard allows you to send, receive, and monitor your assets, as well as access advanced features like CoinJoin for enhanced privacy.

 

By following the steps on Trezor.io/start, you'll have a securely set-up Trezor device, ready to protect your digital assets.
